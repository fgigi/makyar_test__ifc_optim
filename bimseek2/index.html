Studies on Retrieval and Clustering of BIM Components based on Attributes Similarity Measurement

(b) the result of query by keywords
Abstract:

In recent years, with the rapid development and popularization of Building Information Modeling (BIM) in construction engineering field, BIM library resources are growing rapidly on the Web. How to retrieve the appropriate BIM component among so many resources becomes increasingly urgent. Classified catalog browsing, keyword matching and shape matching are the most common BIM component retrieval methods. However, these methods still lack the extraction of BIM component domain information, which leads to the retrieval results are difficult to meet the needs of users. To solve the above problems, we studies on the retrieval and clustering of BIM components based on the domain information of BIM components to support the fast search and reuse in architectural design. The central work consists of three parts as follows. Firstly, we propose a similarity measure algorithm which combines the domain information of BIM component and Tversky similarity measure algorithm. Secondly, we propose a retrieval method called query by model which the user upload a query BIM model and a sorted result of similar BIM components is returned. Thirdly, we propose a BIM component library clustering method, and applied the clustering results to the display of retrieval results.
